Ways you can help

If you are interested in contribution to our project there are several ways to do that.

Bug Reports

If you are good in finding bugs or you've just found the one, feel free to write `em down to our repo's issues section. Please remember that bug reports are good, but good bug reports are much better. Try to follow the guidelines about writing qualitative bug reports.

Feature Requests

We are looking forward to develop and elaborate Just tool, so we welcome new ideas about how to make it more useful for others. Please use issues section to put down your thoughts about new features.

Code & Research

If you are interested in writing code for MSA please refer our main principles and philosophy page for more info about how to write the code, styling, etc.

Money

Donations are welcome. Individuals and companies can donate funds to support Just by PayPal.
